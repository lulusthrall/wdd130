Valuation Guide

What is TCGplayer?

TCGplayer.com is a massive online marketplace that connects thousands of individual hobby stores and sellers with buyers. The "Market Price" listed on their site is an average of what that card has actually sold for recently.

Condition is Everything

A "Base Set Charizard" isn't just a "Base Set Charizard." Its value can range from $20 to $20,000 based on condition.

  • Near Mint (NM): A nearly perfect card.
  • Lightly Played (LP): Minor wear, small scuffs.
  • Damaged (DMG): Deep scratches, creases, or bends.

How to Value Your Own Collection

  1. Identify the Card: Find the name and the set symbol.
  2. Assess the Condition: Be honest! Look under bright light.
  3. Check the Price: Search TCGplayer and filter by condition.
