Valuation Guide

What is TCGplayer?

TCGplayer.com is a massive online marketplace that connects thousands of individual hobby stores and sellers with buyers. The "Market Price" is the industry standard.

Condition is Everything

Click on a condition below to see the grading criteria.

Definition: A nearly perfect card. May have a tiny speck of white on an edge, but otherwise looks like it just came out of the pack.

Value Impact: 100% of Market Price.

Definition: Has minor wear, like small scuffs or light whitening on the corners and edges. No major scratches or bends.

Value Impact: ~70-85% of Market Price.

Definition: Has obvious wear, such as deep scratches, creases, bends, or water damage.

Value Impact: ~10-30% of Market Price.

How to Value Your Own Collection

  1. Identify the Card: Find the name and the set symbol.
  2. Assess the Condition: Be honest! Look under bright light.
  3. Check the Price: Search TCGplayer and filter by condition.
